Got questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you deliver to? +
We currently deliver to all areas in Accra, Tema, Kumasi, and Takoradi. We're expanding to more cities soon! Delivery is always free.
How fresh is the produce? +
Our produce is harvested on the same day you order. Farmers pick in the early morning (5–7 AM), it's quality-checked and packed, then dispatched for delivery the same day. You can't get fresher than that!
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept MTN MoMo, Telecel Cash, AirtelTigo Money, debit/credit cards (Visa & Mastercard), and Pay on Delivery. Mobile money payments come with a 2% discount!
What if I'm not happy with my order? +
We have a 100% freshness guarantee. If any item is damaged, spoiled, or not to your expectation, contact us within 24 hours and we'll replace it or issue a full refund. No questions asked.
How do subscriptions work? +
Choose a farm box (Standard, Office, Soup Starter, or Organic Premium), pick your delivery frequency (weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly), and your box will arrive on schedule. You can pause or cancel anytime with 48 hours notice.
